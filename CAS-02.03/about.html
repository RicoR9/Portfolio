About The Band

Cigarettes After Sex is an American ambient pop band formed in El Paso, Texas, in 2008 by Greg Gonzalez. The band is known for their ethereal, dream-like sound, with their music often featuring reverb-soaked guitars, simple-yet-effective basslines, and Gonzalez's gentle, androgynous vocals.

Their music blends elements of ambient pop, slowcore, and dream pop to create a distinctively atmospheric sound that has drawn comparisons to artists like Mazzy Star, Cocteau Twins, and Chris Isaak. The band's lyrics often explore themes of romance, sexuality, and nostalgia, with Gonzalez drawing from personal experiences to craft intimate narratives.

"I've always wanted to live in the tone of a black and white film."
— Greg Gonzalez

After releasing their first EP, "I.", in 2012, the band gained wider recognition when their song "Nothing's Gonna Hurt You Baby" became popular on YouTube. Their self-titled debut album was released in June 2017 to critical acclaim, followed by their second album "Cry" in 2019, and their third album "X's" in 2023.

The band's minimalist approach to both their music and visual aesthetic has cultivated a devoted following and a distinctive artistic identity in the contemporary indie music scene.
